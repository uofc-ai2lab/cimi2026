About Brain Hack II 2026

São Paulo – Alberta Brain Hack II: Multimodal Medical Image Analysis 2026 brings together researchers, trainees, and professionals working at the intersection of AI and medical image analysis. The workshop will feature invited talks, methodological discussions, and a hands-on MRI classification challenge. Participants will explore the entire workflow—from data acquisition and preprocessing to model development, validation, and interpretability.

Key Topics

  • Medical imaging data acquisition and preprocessing
  • AI model development and reproducible workflows
  • Best practices for training and evaluating ML models
  • Interpretability and explainable AI in medical imaging
  • MRI-based multi-class classification

Hands-On AI Challenge

Participants will work with a curated brain MRI dataset for a multi-class classification task. Teams will build models, evaluate performance, and present their findings.
