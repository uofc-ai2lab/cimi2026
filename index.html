About Brain Hack II 2026

São Paulo – Alberta Brain Hack II: Responsible AI for Medical Imaging 2026 brings together researchers, trainees, and professionals working at the intersection of AI and medical image analysis. The workshop will feature invited talks, methodological discussions, and a hands-on MRI classification challenge. Participants will explore the entire workflow—from data acquisition and preprocessing to model development, validation, and interpretability.

Participants will work with a curated brain MRI dataset for a multi-class classification task. Teams will build models, evaluate performance, and present their findings.
